Master of Arts

Master of Arts, commonly known as MA is a postgraduate degree program that can be pursued in a wide range of specializations. The duration of this course is 2 years offered by universities all over the world. On successful completion of this program successfully, the candidate is bequeathed with a postgraduate degree in MA. You can pursue a full-time course or a So the study in this course includes the topics as per your chosen specialization. There is an correspondence one. This course is available in a massive range of disciplines such as MA in English, Mathematics, Music, Dance, Accountancy, Drama, Economics, Visual effects and many more. array of career options upon completion of MA and that too depends on your specialization. Mastering an Art will improve one knowledge widely on the discipline. Either it may be a language or a subject, it helps the person to know the culture and makes him/her change it depending on the growth.

Eligibility

To be Eligible for the Master of Arts program, the candidate is required to hold the undergraduate degree from a recognized university. There is a simple provision of direct admission in the course and the candidates will be selected as per their merits in previous courses.

Syllabus

Master of Arts program has a sharp scope in various fields as per the course specialization. Candidates with MA in English or mathematics can work as professors in school or colleges or can start their own coaching institutes. Students with other specializations also have various career opportunities that can provide high pay scales. They can work as ounselors in government as well as private organizations, schools, universities, and hospitals, etc.

Along with the increase of start-ups in India, there is a rapid increase in the employment ratio, which means graduates have decent work opportunities after completing their MA.The national, multinational and all other types of companies look for smart postgraduates in various specializations and also they offer good salaries too.

On the other hand, some more career options for MA postgraduates include diving into your own business in a particular specialization. On successful completion of the Master of Arts, postgraduates become eligible for Ph.D., MPhil and many other higher education programs.

The candidates can apply for the jobs in abroad as the Master of Arts postgraduates are in high demand in multinational companies abroad. They can easily get a job as a banker, hospital staff, private companies and other organizations in the UK, the US, UAE, and many other countries which offer relatively high pay scales. They can also apply for foreign higher study programs such as research which further improves the career upon having a doctorate.
