MD Unani

Course

Unani is a traditional healing philosophy. According to Unani the disease is treated as per the symptoms. An illness must be treated separately and dealt with one organ purely based on the symptoms. Master of Medicine in Unani is also known as the MD- Unani and MS Jarahat. It is the purest art of healing which is termed as a noble way of healing for many centuries. Taking Unani treatment as a career is also one of the most satisfying work in the country. The course is a little bit tougher than any other medical courses. There is also a pinch of history involved in it and many terms of Sanskrit are used in it. The treatment process is very cheap and many poor people likely to get treatment. If you are having the habit of helping and treating helpful and needy, then it is a perfect medical course for you.
