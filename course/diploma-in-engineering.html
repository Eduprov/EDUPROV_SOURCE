Diploma in Engineering

Diploma in Engineering is a lucrative option after 10th standard. A three years long diploma in Engineering or industrial courses equips the students to learn the practical and theoretical knowledge needed to do well in the field of Engineering and take up amazing jobs in the industry. Completing the course also allows students to join the 2nd year in Bachelor of Engineering or Bachelors of Technology courses. The hands-on approach from this course will help the student and give them an extra edge when they go into positions after their education.

The basic eligibility to get into a polytechnic course, students need to have completed class 10 or SSLC with a minimum of 35 per cent marks and pass class

Students can choose to apply for colleges individually or through the state department common application example, DCET for polytechnic colleges in Karnataka. There are several polytechnic subjects that students can choose from depending on their interest and strengths.
