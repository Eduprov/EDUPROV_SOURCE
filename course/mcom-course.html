Master of Commerce

Master of Commerce commonly known as M. Com is a postgraduate program in Commerce, finance, economics and accounts related aspects. The duration of this master’s degree course is 2 years. Upon satisfying the eligibility criteria one can know further about how to join in this course and what are the advantages of being an M. Com graduate.On successful completion of the coursework, the candidate will be honored with a postgraduate degree of M. Com. In this course, students learn the skills like corporate management and marketing, business responsibilities and Finance management, etc. that make them job ready right after the course completion. The candidates can work in the fields of human resource, research and development, accounting, finance and many more.

For this coursework, the candidates are required to choose one or two specializations as per their interest areas. You have options to choose from various business subjects. While following the coursework, you study about diverse topics and subjects such as Corporate Accounting, Business Economics, Financial and Service Management, Tax Management and Planning, Marketing and Entrepreneurship etc.

To be eligible for M.Com coursework, the aspirants must have a graduation degree in Commerce. Both modes, direct admissions and through entrance exam are available for admission in this course. Colleges and universities providing direct admissions usually demand 60% marks in the graduation course. reas, the admission process through entrance exam purely depends on the aspirant’s merits in the said entrance exam.

The M.Com postgraduate course has an immense scope all over the world. The candidates have job options of various types. Most population career options for M.Com postgraduates include Accountants, Finance, Banking, Operations management and jobs in the insurance industry. The candidates become eligible for various civil services and can apply for higher studies for the advancement of their career. Also, Traditional academic professorship opportunities are also open to these postgraduates.

With the plethora of national and international companies in India, the accounting job opportunities are on the rise in the recent era. Along with accounting professionals, these companies hire M.Com candidates for the posts of business analyst, corporate analyst, cashier and for sale & purchase department. Starting own company can lead to more revenues and success to these candidates.

The companies in abroad (mainly the US, the UK, UAE, France and Japan) welcome M.Com professionals from across the world, which means these candidates have safe and secure career opportunities with higher pay scales in abroad. The Financial Planning, Chartered Financial Analyst, Investment Advisor and Venture capitalist are some of the most popular job profiles being offered to M.Com candidates in abroad. On gaining the expertise in your work field, the candidates will be able to get an attractive pay scale. Career after this M.Com coursework has extreme growth with decent income options.
