Bachelor of Mass Communication (BMC)

Course Information

In the contemporary world, there are many courses that are offered at the undergraduate level, but there are only a few courses that offer you a high-paying scale and that can aid you in having a prosperous future. Among-st such courses, the name of Mass Communication is among high-level pay package courses. This course demands versatility and flexibility and also your full capacity to mold the news and stuff related to it in such a way that it attracts more and more people to your work.

This course is the direct reflection of the media line and exposure of the journalism. This course fully converts one into a real professional in which one just owns the ability to mold the truth and expose the culprits, in front of the world.Exposure of truth, worldly matters, sports, and much more options are there in this line that is quite intriguing and enchanting. This line has its own gravity as people can forget to have their morning tea, but they can’t forget to see the newspapers and news channels. So, this line is so wide that one can envision its nice and companionable network of people.

Eligibility

One can pursue this course at both levels, one at graduation and second at post-graduation. This course seldom requires any stipulation other than the good communication skill. Here is the list of a few things that one needs to have to commence the journey in this line.

  • One needs to have a passing certificate of high school and intermediate and if going to the renowned colleges,
  • One needs to have an aggregate of 50% in intermediate.
  • one requires having a graduation degree from a recognized university (any stream).
  • The minimum age limit is 18 years and for post-graduation, there is no specific age limit prescribed.

Scope

This course is not at all confined as it has got so many branches and the future of this course is quite perforating but it requires the working people, who should have the capability to work with dedication. In the national arena, this course is getting viral as the news and articles have become an essential part of everyone’s life. These things keep people up-to-date about daily matters and whatever is happening in the world. From the international point of view, this is a great opportunity for those, who have a great command of their accent and words as there are so many countries that invite the intriguing writers, who can write about the burning issues.

If one is trying for an internship, then they can find a very less pay scale as people don't wish to pay more in the initial stage and probably one will be hired at 10k monthly. If direct placement is there then one might get 20k to 35k per-months. If one gets the perfect platform like the big news channels, then no need to think of money as there one gets the name and fame both the things in hand. So, this line is quite interesting for the adventurous people, if you have the dare then tries it.
