B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering

The Aeronautical Engineering branch in the field of engineering, which is involved in building up new technology in the field of space exploration, aviation and defense systems. Aeronautical Engineering deals with the research, design, development, construction, testing, science, and technology of aircraft. In India, Aeronautical Engineering offers an exciting career, especially for those who love to fly. Aeronautical Colleges Admissions in India is the perfect platform for wannabe Aeronautical Engineers. The aviation sector is not only very challenging but also there is a huge scope of development and expansion in the coming future.

Aeronautical or aerospace engineering is related to the development and day-to-day growth of new technologies in the field of space exploration, defense systems, and the aviation industry. This course offer candidate to specialize in designing, development, construction, operation, maintenance and testing of both military and commercial aircraft, satellites, missiles, spacecraft, and their components as well. It is known as Aerospace Engineering. Generally, the Aeronautics side of the Aerospace Industry has four major divisions which include civilian aircraft engines, aircraft, military aircraft, and missile systems. The Aeronautical Engineer can design, develop, and test aircraft, satellites, missiles and other aeronautical systems.

  • To become an Aeronautical Engineer you should have a degree of B.E. / B.Tech or diploma in Aeronautics.
  • The basic requirement for B.E. / B Tech is 10+2 (HSC), PUC or equivalent with Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics, and the most important thing fairly high percentage aggregate in 10+2 examination.
  • You must need to qualify for entrance exams.

The job opportunities for aeronautical engineers are available at national, international, private, and public firms. Degree holders can find jobs in air turbine production plants or in the aviation industry for designing, developing, and testing programs. The aeronautical environment is sophisticated with opportunities for rewarding careers involving leading-edge technology. Most of the Aeronautical Engineers are working in ISRO (Indian Space research Organization) and the Defense Ministry in India. Job opportunity for aeronautical engineers is also available in Defense Research and Development Laboratories (DRDO), National Aeronautical Laboratory, Civil Aviation Department, and Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL). In India, various job opportunities for Aeronautical Engineers are available across the country, they can work with various airlines with different Airlines and Flying clubs, private and government air service and aircraft manufacturers, DRDO, ISRO, NAL, etc. This course also has a wide range of opportunities abroad.
