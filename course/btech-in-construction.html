B.Tech in Construction Engineering

Construction Management (CM) is an undergraduate course in the field of construction. In this course a candidate studies about the proper technique of construction using methods of engineering and business education. Construction is one sector that is always in a boom. There are people who are having experience, but not certified. In the course of CM, a degree holder gets the certification and experience to work in any type of environment. With the course, a person will get a detailed concept of managing the construction site on their own.

The construction managers are also known as the project managers where their crucial role is to supervise and coordinate the workforce. The work assigned for a construction manager is to manage a group of laborers who are working on the construction sites. The construction phase of the project is controlled by the construction manager.
