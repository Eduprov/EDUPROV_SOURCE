Master of Engineering (M.E)

Course

Master of Engineering (M.E) is a postgraduate program in the fields of Engineering and technology. The course is for 2 years and to get avail of this course you should have an undergraduate degree in Bachelors in Engineers or Technology. There are many institutions in the country which are providing a good foundation to get a Masters Degree in Engineering.

Master of Engineering is also termed as Master of Technology, Master of Science in Technology, and Master of Science in Engineering. This degree is few steps lesser than Ph.D. and considered somewhat equal to M. Phil. and M. Sc. Degrees. This program is an advanced course for anyone who wants to take a level of engineering to a different level.

There is not one program but many courses that come under M.E includes the fields of Architectural Engineering, Aviation, Building Service, Civil Engineering, and Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Technology, Electrical Engineering, Product Development, Electronics, Engineering, Engineering Design, Mechanical Engineering, Information Systems, Information Technology, Navigation, Robotics, Telecommunication and Wireless Communication.

Eligibility

  • There is no age limit to avail Engineering in Masters Program. where several Universities carry out the education system of Masters Degree.

There are many institutions in India from which a person who has completed a Bachelors Degree in Engineering in their particular field are able to take part in this program. To start with the course a candidate has to go through an entrance exam. Different states conduct different entrances for admission and there is also a common entrance GATE. The score of the GATE exam not only makes you to get into the college, but also have several other benefits such as getting into the government sector, stipend, etc.

Scope

An engineer is required in every field where technical work is required. After the course is completed, a person is able to work with managing different projects. They are also able to work alone using their engineering degree. They are also able to take different projects which are out of government organizations. Every area needs a specialist engineer even other countries. There are various colleges in aboard from which a candidate is able to complete the course and get a job there.
