B.Tech Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE)

The B.Tech Electrical and Electronic Engineering or Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) comprises the study of electricity and electrical circuits. This course is a modern course and is available since the 19th century after the commercialization of electrical products. This course mainly deals with the study of electrical and electronic devices and the application of the devices.

It is one of the favorite and most promising educational courses in the fields of engineering. In the course, a student gains knowledge about electronic appliances and study about devices and power.

The scope of this course is vast and it exists in hydro energy, Fuel Cells, geothermal energy, turbine, solar, or gas. Not only an aspirant studies about the devices but they also learn about modern equipment. They also gain deeper knowledge in Mechanical fields, electronics, Communications, and Computers. More information will be provided by the experts of EDUPROV. Complete assistance will be provided by them right from the eligibility criteria to the counseling.

Bachelor’s Course: The minimum educational qualification for applying the course is to pass 10+2. In the 12 the minimum pass marks are 45% with taking Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Computer and also English. The student has to qualify in JEE or any entrance exams and go through a college interview. The duration of the course is for 4 years. The person with a diploma course can also enter into this course, as a direct second year as a lateral entrant. A Diploma is a basic course that can be acquired just after completing 10th or 12th. The duration of the diploma course is for 3 years.

Master’s Course: The Master’s Degree is EEE can only be attained after the successful completion of a Bachelor’s Degree in the same field (i.e.,) Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The second Criteria is to qualify the GATE exam. The duration of this program is for 2 years.

The scope of EEE has a wide range of job opportunities. They are liable to work in airspace, manufacturing industries, Telecom Industries, IT industries, Railways and Transportation Networks. After getting the Master’s Degree a candidate can also start teaching. There are private sectors which offer a great deal of job profile like Network Engineer, IT consultant, Systems analyst, Broadcast Engineer, System developer, and various others. When one completes a master degree they can opt for research. The easiest way to get placed in a multinational foreign company is through campus selection. The other way is to find jobs online and only a resume is enough to apply online for the job.
