B.Sc Forensic Medicine and Toxicology

B.Sc Forensic Medicine and Toxicology is the science of applying medical facts to legal problems. B.Sc. Forensic Medicine and Toxicology is a three year undergraduate course in which the technologies and the facts of forensic medicine will be revealed to successfully discover and overcome the various crimes that occurs in the world.
