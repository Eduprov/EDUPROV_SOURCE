B.Tech Mining Engineering

Mining engineering right from the name you can know what the course is about. Yes!! If you choose this course you will be a mining engineer where you will apply the latest technology and science to extract the minerals from the earth. While following the course, you study some basic subjects like physics, geology, chemistry, environmental studies, mathematics and economics followed by technical subjects like mine design, mine ventilation, mineral processing, surveying, rock fragmentation, rock mechanics and resource law. Mining engineering also helps in converting an exhausted mine site into a safe and livable habitat.

Bachelor of Technology (B. Tech.) or Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Mining is an undergraduate course for the study of science and technologies related to the extraction and location of minerals beneath the earth safely and efficiently. The duration of the course is 4 years offered in engineering colleges. After completing this course successfully, the student is bequeathed with an engineering degree in Mining. Like genetic or marine engineering this course is not common. From this, you can know that this course is not available in all engineering colleges. Only a few colleges offer this course but this course has a good scope. We at EDUPROV have a separate panel where they will guide you about the colleges that provide this course along with the ranking of the institution.

  • To be eligible for the course, one must have qualified 10+2 in Non-Medical stream (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics).

Mining Engineering has a very broad scope in India. Mining Engineers are well employed in the mining sectors fulfilling the roles of mines inspector, mine designer, technician, supervisors and safety inspectors. Some of the posts like mining engineers, geotechnical engineers, and mining technicians are also in demand. A Mining Engineer can also work as a lecturer in the engineering college, but to get on to higher levels one must procure the degree of M.E./M.Tech in mining or Ph.D./ doctoral degree.

There is a sharp rise in demand for Mining Engineers in abroad. You can apply for the jobs or can go for Research in foreign universities.
