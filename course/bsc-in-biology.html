B.Sc Biology

B.Sc Biology or Bachelor of Science in Biology is an undergraduate degree course of 3years. Biology is the combination of Botany and Zoology as we all know as we heard that right from our school days. B.Sc Biology is somehow a detailed study of infinite nature. If you love nature and want to explore more about nature then you can opt for this course. This course has numerous advantages and will provide knowledge about living organisms.

Biology is a natural science that is concerned with the study of life and living organisms, including their structure, function, growth, origin, evolution, distribution, and taxonomy, function, properties of the processes of all forms of life.

Subjects such as Botany, Biochemistry, Classification of Animal & Plant Kingdom, Zoology, Plant Diversity & Physiology, and Genetics and more will be there in this course which provides you a wider knowledge in both plants and animals.

Biology is about life so the scope of this course doesn't diminish until the earth revolves. One who completes this course has several job opportunities and higher studies. This course has several advantages. If you are the one who scored less in 10+2 and lose your cut off for medicine, no need to worry this course provides you an opportunity. Yes!! After completion of this course, one can do MBBS in some specific institutions. More details about the benefits can be obtained in detail from the dedicated professionals of the EDUPROV.

  • Candidates need to fulfill certain conditions that include securing at least 50% marks in 10+2 from a recognized board.
  • Students must have opted for Physics, Chemistry and Biology subjects in 10+2.
  • Some institutions select students on the basis of the Merit list, which is based on the scorecard of 10+2, personal interview, capabilities, and skills.
  • Complete assistance will be provided by the experts of the EDUPROV in case of the admission and the counseling.

B.Sc. Biology degree gives an opportunity for higher studies in this field like M.Sc., Ph.D., and M.Phil. degree in Botany, Zoology, Food Science, Biomedical Sciences, Virology, etc. Biology graduates have opportunities in the field of Research & Development, Pharmaceuticals & Chemical industries, Public Health, and Environmental Protection Sectors, Clinical Research, Waste Management, etc.Students can go to foreign countries for their higher education.

They can shine in various fields such as Biology Research Assistant, Lecturer in Clinical Embryology and Zoology, Biology Content Developer, Botanist, Taxonomist, etc.
