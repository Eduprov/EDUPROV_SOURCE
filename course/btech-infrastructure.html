B.Tech Infrastructure Engineering

Course

B.Tech Infrastructure Engineering deals with the concept of creating a better infrastructure of buildings, roads, transportation routes, townships, environmental issues, and various others. During the course of study, a student learns how to tackle extreme situations. There are regular classes with field projects to give a deeper sense of understanding about the course. By the name of the course Infrastructure Engineering, you may come to the conclusion that this course is the same as the civil engineering. Doing Infrastructure engineering and civil engineering is the same. But both are different. All infrastructure engineers are civil engineers, but all civil engineers are not infrastructure engineers. A civil engineer to be an infrastructure engineer want to do a master's in infrastructure engineering.

Within the duration of the course, the framework is adopted for emphasizing on the operations of connected infrastructure, designing, planning, and management. There are other environmental factors like wind, geography, and water that have to consider while working. In this field, there is always a term for bringing new innovation and changes in infrastructure. More information about the course and the institutions which provide this course can be obtained in detail from the EDUPROV's expert when you reach/contact us.

  • Bachelor of Technology in Infrastructure Engineering or B. Tech. (Infrastructure Engineering) is a 4-year degree course.
  • To get avail in this course an aspirant has to complete 10+2 with Math, Chemistry, and Physics with at least 45% marks.
  • Major colleges are having their own terms of the Entrance Exam. Some of the colleges provide entrance exam on the basis of Merit List.
