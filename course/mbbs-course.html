Bachelor of Medicine & Bachelor of Surgery

There are many of you in that crowd of thousands and lakhs who are service-minded, who want to serve others, who want to save humanity and probably want to do something great in life. Are you one amongst them who loves to know about human body, how it functions and its systematic elements. Are you service-minded? Do you love to know about what is happening in the human body? Do you love to save a life? Then MBBS is the apt course. The Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) is a very challenging but tremendously rewarding course. It gives you complete medical knowledge, research, and clinical skills, awareness, enthusiasm and attitudes for a profession in any field of medicine. Yes by doing this course you`ll be self- satisfied and you`ll have that upper edge in the society, and amongst everyone who knows you. By working in this field, you`ll experience helping several patients who are suffering from diseases every day. You`ll work in a different environment and will have to communicate or come across various sets of people. MBBS or Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery is one of the most popular degrees among the students. The job demand is very high and the field is very competitive owing to the fact that the field is difficult to pursue, demand is high and the criticality of it is high. It consists of 2 bachelor degrees in a single degree upon successfully completing this degree you can practice as a doctor. The medical course in India is regulated by the Medical Council of India or MCI.

MBBS is also known as Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery is one of the most prestigious, esteemed, and highly regarding courses in the science stream. MBBS aims to give an academic understanding of many concepts all relating to human body and its whereabouts. In India MBBS is the most sought out courses and lakhs of students compete in MBBS admissions every year. Nonetheless MBBS seats in India are very limited and the students have to face cut throat competition in order to get admissions. MBBS aims to give an academic and in-depth understanding of concepts such as human anatomy, chemistry, medicine, drugs formulation, pharmaceutical experience and methods of surgery through practical and theoretical experiences. Lest it isn`t an easy job. Well, it is a very challenging job.!

  • The person should complete 10+2 education with a minimum aggregate score of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as main subjects. In the case of reservation, the minimum score drops to 45 %.
  • The lower age limit for MBBS admission and entrance the exam is 17 years while the upper age limit is 25 years
  • One should have qualifying marks in the entrance exams like National level tests like NEET or AIIMS. 
  • The rank obtained in this exam determines the selection of students for pursuing a medical degree. 
  • There are no restrictions on the maximum NEET attempt limit. 

In order to get into this challenging course one needs to attempt the grueling entrance exam NEET.. These entrance exams determine the ranking of students allowing them to apply for MBBS in different institutes and colleges. Admissions to all central, deemed, private, and government MBBS colleges and universities are done based on the NEET scores. To win a place in one of those 66,500 MBBS seats in India, over 15 lakh students undertake the entrance exams. Only a small percentage gets admitted into an MBBS course making it one of the most competitive and challenging degrees to pursue. NEET is also known as the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test is an exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). This is what aspiring students who are in the long run of becoming a doctor should take up. So this is the only getaway for aspirants to get admissions which will land them in the country`s top colleges and universities. NEET 2021 Registration and Application forms are expected to be released anytime soon NEET Exam Duration: The duration of the exam is 3 hours NEET Question Pattern: There are 180 objective type questions with four options per question.

After completing their MBBS the student may use the title `Dr` with their name. They can practice medicine either by starting their own clinic or by getting a job in the government sector or private sector. They can work in numerous places such as medical colleges, hospitals, nursing homes, health departments, health ministry, health corporations, plus they can also become researchers, scientists, professors and lecturers. This course has a high demand and has wide options in India. But other than the huge demand, the field is graced with recent advancements in technology. This has opened up numerous amount of opportunities for students in the medical field. You may choose to work in general practice and primary health care, or choose from the wide range of other specialist disciplines such as Neurology, Gynaecology, Paediatrics, Obstetrics, Dermatology, Pathology, Psychiatry, Oncology, General medicine, Emergency medicine, Public health, Geriatric medicine, Medical research, and education or Radiology and surgery. Studying for an MBBS degree is a very gruesome and sapping process but the end result is 100% placement rate. It is a very fulfilling and significant career step to becoming a doctor and securing and saving lives.
