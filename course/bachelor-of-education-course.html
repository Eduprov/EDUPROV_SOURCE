Bachelor of Education

Course Information

Education changes everyone's life. For a human being, education is present in each and every stage of his /her life. Education has been present from ancient times and has been seen throughout the history of the world. It has transformed into various phases at various stages depending upon the development. In this era, education is highly developed and everything can be learned by a click.

B. Ed. or Bachelor of Education is a bachelor's degree course at the undergraduate level. It is offered by a lot of colleges or universities all over the country. It is a course for students who intend to work as teachers. Teaching is a respected job in India and the teachers focus on various subjects and areas. The course is a specialized course and the minimum eligibility for joining the course is that the student must have done an undergraduate degree course in some discipline. In India, it is obligatory to have a B.Ed degree in order to serve as a teacher in high schools as well as higher primary schools. For completing the course, the aspirants should at least complete their graduation with a B.A, B.Com, or B.Sc degree The course is also accessible at many open universities through distance learning and here the duration is a minimum of 2 years and a maximum of 5 years.

The National Council for Teacher Education is the solitary authority that is accountable for observing the programs on education, including B.Ed.

ELIGIBILITY

To be eligible for the B.Ed. course one must hold a BA / B.Sc / B. Com degree from a recognized University/Board with at least 50% marks in aggregate.

Aspirants need to appear in an entrance test held by Universities and various states and also independent bodies. Admission to various colleges and educational institutions is offered according to the rank of the students in the respective entrance tests. This is a highly competitive sector and requires dedicated preparation for cracking the entrance. Not to fear when EDUPROV is here we will help you at each and every step to Admission and to join in the course.

Scope

Before taking up any course/career, the usual question that arises in every person’s mind is the scope of that course/curriculum; questions like, “Can I get a job after this course?” and “What are the areas of diversification after finalizing this course?”

After finishing B. Ed, there are a lot of possibilities to get employed in government, in addition to private schools, with a decent salary package. The remuneration depends upon your post in the school.

If you are economically strong and have the ability to run a school, then the second option is to open your own school. In the beginning, you can start a lesser school and afterward, with the help of loans and finances of banks and other financial institutions you can expand it to set up a new, bigger school.

Interested aspirants can also follow higher studies after completing B.Ed. They can pursue M.Ed or Master of Education, which is the higher specialized postgraduate course after the accomplishment of B.Ed. After successful completion of M.Ed., students can do a Ph.D.

Teaching is a respected profession in this country and also in the entire world. There is a demand for teachers both government and private sectors. After completion of B.Ed, candidates shall be entitled to get employment in secondary as well as higher secondary schools. With a B.Ed degree candidates can get jobs in Schools, Coaching Centers, Education consultancies, Education departments, private tuition, etc. The remuneration will be augmented with a decent experience and accomplishment in the field of education.
