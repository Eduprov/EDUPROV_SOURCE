Overview

Singapore is one of the largest portions in South East Asia and is one of the over-ornate in the world. It is also a member of the Commonwealth program after being declared as an independent state. Singapore is a city with almost 64 islands and is the most visited tourist destination. With its world class eminent facilities in education system and world-renowned universities, it is abode to thousands of international students most preferred study abroad destinations. The country has provided a global platform for students to explore all fields and choose a desired career in their preferred domain. Singapore is a safe city with a low crime rate and a stable political environment and the cleanest Country.

Being fairly close to India, Singapore encourages students to visit their home during every academic break. The country offers diverse fields of options that students can easily choose and is a premier education hub offering a global perspective towards education. It has well-developed infrastructure and is acknowledged to be a global business hub with consistency in political stability and business policies open enough to offer students many career opportunities.

It has gone surpassing all odds and has been delivering quality education to all students. Besides this it has an adult literacy rate of 97%. Singapore is a student friendly city which encourages students to pursue education and also work part time. International students are permitted to work for 16 hours a week alongside attending University. Global reports have recognized Singapore as a world pioneer in research and innovation. And the city in itself has ranked 15th in the QS Best student cities 2018 giving a thorough justification of its educational excellence, high quality of life and a very diverse student community.

Almost every MNC has its Asia‐Pacific Head Office in Singapore making it easier for students to find jobs. i.e., Home to more than 7000 MNC’s. Singapore is a nation built by immigrants and it still welcomes all who are keen to play a part in its economic development. International students are normally not permitted to work part time during their studies. However, they can apply for a work permit after their studies are completed and if they have found a job. There are a few exceptions to this rule. In case the students are enrolled in a course that has a built-in internship the students can work during that time. On a case to case basis student can get 1-month extension + 1 additional extension of 15 days is possible. Full time work after completion of studies: For a Bachelor Graduate the salary range can vary from S$ 1800 to S$ 2500 per month. For a Masters Graduate the salary range can vary from S$ 2000 to S$ 3000 per month.

Education System

Originally based on the British model, the Singaporean education system has evolved to meet the needs of the globalised economy. It has moved from a fairly rigid orientation to incorporate emphases like personalisation of learning, advanced technology, innovation, and creativity.

The universities in Singapore often feature in lists of the top universities in the world. There are currently 34 universities in Singapore, of which six are national and government-funded. There are also foreign universities in Singapore which have either set up a physical campus (institutes of higher learning) or have joint collaborations/programmes with local universities (local tie-ups).

Why study in Singapore?

Top notch education system: Singapore is one amongst the other top popular study abroad destination because of its exceptional contribution in education and career opportunities for students. Singapore is the most desirable and preferred educational destination in the Asian estate. Being the busiest city in the world with all hustle and bustle, Singaporeans education system has drastically developed and has well adopted the western ideologies and methodologies. Universities in Singapore are gaining world wide reputation and recognition. Two of Singapore`s leading universities are ranked within world`s Top 15. There are almost 34 universities in Singapore out of which 6 are national. Feature the world-renowned National University of Singapore (NUS), and Nanyang Technological University (NTU) both are featured in the QS World University Ranking of 2021.

Multicultural environment: There are different cultures and many sets of people where they respect each other religions. There are no language barriers in the city. Students can easily dwell in the culture as there are four languages in Singapore- English, Chinese, Malay and Tamil. English is considered as the most used in schools, colleges and government places. The mix of multi-cultural ethnic values and diversity has enabled Singapore to attract thousands of students from many places across the globe. There are many similarities in the city and other parts of the world as it is one of a popular tourist spot that students can have a pleasant experience throughout their stay.

Clean, Safe and green environment: Singapore has been ranked as the second safest in the world by Safe City Index by EIU in 2019. As laws are strictly enforced in Singapore, everyone needs to adhere to the rules. This has helped in a decrease of crime rates and has increased its reputation of being one of the safest countries in the world. Many international students take this as a plus point as living away from their home in a new country that can cause worry for both the students and their parents. This is why NUS has its own security team patrolling campuses 24/7, 365 days of the year. Students are safe even late at night. And Singapore is the greenest city in Asia.

A city to explore: International students in Singapore are all set to experience a unique and immersive self-experience. Learning and living here is not just confined to the classroom but goes way beyond into the global view. Singapore embraces a rich culture as it is an ancient city with beautiful history, architecture, cuisine, festivities, infrastructure and leisure activities attracting many students. Besides this, the world`s largest human resource management committee agency, which ranked Singapore as the city with a high quality of life in Asia in its 2019 Urban Life Quality Survey having an effective and efficient city planning developed economy, convenient transportation and very friendly social life making the city student-friendly and socially adaptable country for International students.

Visa Requirement

The Singapore Government has come up with a specific scheme also named as SOLAR system; Student`s Pass Online Application and Registration system which is an official registration department which is a head in monitoring the student visa application

  1. Proof of financial stability.
  2. Passport all up to date.
  3. Submission of birth certificate and passport size photographs.
  4. A well-executed SOP statement of purpose along with 3 copies and 3 letter of recommendations LOR.
  5. Visa fee receipts along with application form.
  6. All documentation in relevance to former degrees such as academic transcripts and relevance of entrance exams on international level like GRE, GMAT etc.
  7. Travel and health insurance
  8. Fully vaccinated certificates
  9. Letter of admission from the university
  10. Proof of residence and location travel destination.

International students must apply for a Student Pass which can vary depending upon what is studied and where. Students will also need a study visa. They must apply directly to their chosen institution.

  1. Passport size photograph in white background.
  2. ICA‐IMM16 Form : Applicant’s signature MUST match with passport.
  3. ICA‐V36 Form :To declare all applicant’s educational and working history. To submit all supporting documents. All gaps to be filled.
  4. Applicant’s Passport : Expiry date must be more than 6 months.
  5. All supporting documents of foreign languages MUST be officially translated into English.
  6. Applicant’s Birth Certificate: Both parents’ names & applicant’s name must be spelt exactly the same as the names shown in applicant’s passport.
  7. Affidavit of Name (if applicant’s name in BC differs with passport).
  8. Applicant’s Marriage/Divorce Document (if married/divorced).
  9. Applicant/Parent’s Valid Bank Statement/s: Diploma ‐ Rs. 10 Lakhs, PG Diploma ‐ Rs. 10 Lakhs Bachelors ‐ Rs. 20 – 28 Lakhs, Masters ‐ Rs. 20 – 22 Lakhs
  10. One day old funds will also be sufficient and Bank documents must not be issued more than 3 months before
  11. Student must submit full documents preferably 4 weeks before course commencement date.
  12. University/ College will lodge student pass application electronically with ICA (Immigration & Checkpoint Authority)
  13. Associate to ensure student signs on every single page of both the Student Contract & Advisory Note. Associate to scan back to us scan copy of signed documents.
  14. It will take 3‐4 weeks to process student pass application.
  15. Once application is successful ICA issues approval letter.
  16. After receiving approval letter student has to pay 1st installment of tuition fees.
  17. After receiving payment University will issue IPA letter.
  18. University generally allows one week’s extension in case of delay in Visa.
  19. IPA letter is valid for 10‐15 days.
  20. The student can book his ticket and can reach Singapore on the basis of IPA.
  21. After reaching Singapore, the student needs to do medical checkup. The student can do the same from India or from Singapore.
  22. After submitting the Medical check‐up report along with all the original documents to ICA, ICA will issue student pass.
