Biotechnology

Biotechnology is the combination of two subjects- Biology and Technology.B.Sc Biotechnology or Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology is a three-year degree program. This course has great scope as it is the course of the future. Biotechnology will continue to grow as the future of human life depends on it right from stem cell research to the discovery of new drugs.

The main aim of this course is to understand the science, technology and business management, along with the entrepreneurial skills required to exploit technological advances within a competitive environment.

This course includes various subjects such as Biochemistry, Molecular & Cellular Biology, Biodiversity, Plants for the Future, Green Biotechnology and Dissertation, etc. This is the perfect course for the one who loves to know about living beings and the various changes and activities that takes place in them. This course has a wide scope in the case of the research. So, one who loves to invent can opt for this course.

Production and Manufacturing sectors are always in demand, thus, B.Sc. graduates are always in demand. After getting a B.Sc. degree, one has various opportunities such as opt for higher studies in the core area or in management courses which will lead to attaining a managerial position. The fee for B.Sc. courses is comparatively low when compared with professional courses like engineering and medicine. To know more about the benefits of getting a B.Sc degree, particularly in Biotechnology you can approach our dedicated professionals of EDUPROV who are always waiting to assist you.

Eligibility

In order to have a B. Sc degree in Biotechnology, the student should be qualified with 50% marks in 10+2 Science stream. Both PCM (Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics) as well as PCB (Physics, Chemistry & Biology) group students are eligible for this course.

Scope

After B.Sc. Technology, students can opt for the Master course as M.Sc. Biotechnology, which will take two years to complete. The scope of this course is high in various fields like Organ Transplantation, Rational Drug Design, Stem Cell techniques, Tissue Culture, Enzyme Engineering, etc.

Biotechnology has a wide scope in abroad. Bio-engineers can find job opportunities in hospitals, research centers, and government agencies and can make a better future.

Biotechnologists can find employment in a wide range of industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing companies, chemical firms, biotech firms, and breweries
