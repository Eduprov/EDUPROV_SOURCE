Bachelor of Mental Retardation

Mental Retardation is a condition where the exact root cause is almost unknown. A person can lead to mental retardation due to their health issues, any genetic disorders, complications during birth, and pregnancy.Surrounding influences can also be a cause for the same. Such conditions can be observed in patients through their limitations in daily routine life functioning, i.e. lack of intellectual development and curiosity, irregular behavior, immaturity, lack in learning academic parts. However, it can be treated through intense practice to support the patient, i.e. social engagements, taking care of the needs, hobbies, and social skills.

Bachelor of Mental Retardation is a special education academic program so that the practitioners can identify the special needs of patients and build confidence in them. Specialized, who is equipped with this degree, can help to develop the skills and competencies in an effective way. The BMR is available in two categories. A five-year degree course and the one-year academic program. The 5-year degree course vailable in institutions like Osmania University,AP and the one-year academic program offered by many of the reputed institutes in India i.e.

  • Indira Gandhi Institute of Co-operative Management, UP.
  • Anna University, TN.

Eligibility

  • 5year Undergraduate course: You need to have a minimum qualification of 10+2 in physics, chemistry, and biology to pursue a B.M.R.Sc in any government or private college.
  • Mathematics is not compulsory. You need to secure a minimum of 50% to get admission to this course.
  • The institution may have a separate entrance examination and have different procedures.
  • One year special education: To be BMR in special education, a person needs to possess B.A/B.Sc. or an equivalent degree at graduate cadre from a recognized university.
  • The candidate needs to possess a minimum of 45% aggregate marks in the qualifying degree exam. However, the admission procedure can be decided by the university.
  • You can get complete information about the courses of BRM and the importance and scope of the course from the experts at EDUPROV. We will assist you as per your need in the admission procedure right from applying until the counseling.

Scope

After completion of BMR special education, there is a vast scope of getting employment in India. There are careers in the field of:

  • Educational Domains
  • Personal Assistance and support to individuals who are dealing with MR
  • Rehabilitation Domains
  • NGOs
