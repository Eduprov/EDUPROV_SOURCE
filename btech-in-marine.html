Overview

Bachelor of Technology in Marine Engineering / B.Tech Marine Engineering is one such branch of engineering which is expertise in designing, building and construction of machine equipment's for sea crafts, harbor installation, docks, all sea and water related operations. Marine engineering is a 4 year under graduate course with 8 semesters, exams subsequently being held every 6 months. This comes with practical sessions and theoretical sessions which provide students a co-operative method of learning.

Marine Engineering is the field that studies about the production, designing, manufacturing, maintenance and operations of all ship mechanisms. It deals with both the internal and external technicalities of a body that sails on water. Many years ago, Marine engineering would have not even been known to many engineers but today, it is an established profession by many.

There are many mechanisms that help in the operation or a ship like the electricity. Power generation system, propulsion mechanism, lubrication, water distillation, lighting and air conditioning system etc. All these activities are being taken care by the marine engineers who specialize in many fields. This engineering is sub ordinately linked with other engineering branches like mechanical, civil, electrical & electronics, computers etc. one such branch is that of marine engineering.

Eligibility criteria

  1. Candidates must have completed their 10+2 from a recognized university or institution.
  2. Candidates must have completed their 12th with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as mandatory subjects.
  3. Candidates must have completed their 12th with a minimum of 50% in 12th. SC/ST candidates will get a relaxation of 5%.
  4. The admission process is purely merit basis or entrance exam basis and direct admission.

Age Limit: Candidates' age should be in between 17 to 25 Years and SC/ST students will get a relaxation of 5 years.

Medical Eligibility: Physical Fitness- Candidate should be medically fit as per Merchant Shipping (Medical Examination) Rules, 2000, as amended.

Eyesight - Candidates must possess good binocular vision

Admission Process

In order to get admissions in marine engineering, students have to attempt an entrance exam which can be state level, national level or university level. Admissions happen either through entrance exams or through merit- based performance in their 12th. Usually for merit- based performance the cut off percentage would be set quiet high from college to college.

Course Structure

Diploma in Marine Engineering: Diploma in marine engineering is a 3- year diploma program that students can choose after completion of their 10th standard.

B.Tech in Marine engineering: B.E or B.Tech in marine engineering is a 4 year under graduate degree course that students can complete after finishing their 12th standard.

M.Tech in Marine Engineering: M.E or M.Tech in marine engineering is a 2 year post graduate course also known as post- graduation which offers a good career prospect.

Career and Scope

Marine engineering course teaches the aspiring candidates to design, develop, construct sea equipment’s, and sea operating machines. Candidates will be prepared to perform jobs with their skills not just in the field but also in industries of various marine fields like shipping, steel industry, power sector, consultancy firms, manufacturing sector and much more.

With the modernization in the trade sector occurring between countries through water ways, the need for different sea instruments and machines have come into existence. To operate and build in new technologies, to globalize trade and to re amend the usual technologies, young and multi-faceted marine engineers have high demand for jobs. The increase in the employment opportunities has lured many individuals to opt this course as their career. Both the specializations like merchant navy and armed navy have great opportunities for this field.

Marine engineers can swap to being an armed navy foregoing a physical and medical fitness exam in a few countries. Marine engineering is a good field of study for budding young minds to opt and if such interest and skills are put together will definitely go on to leave an ineradicable mark on the flourishing stream.

A marine engineer's job on a ship can be stimulating as the engineer room is a complex mechanical system with a belligerent environment. Thus, they have to acclimatize to the job.
