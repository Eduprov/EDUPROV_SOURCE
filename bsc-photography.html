B.Sc Photography

Bachelors of Science in Photography is a three years long undergraduate degree. This is a crucial step for students who enjoy clicking photographs and telling visual stories. This course will help them sharpen their photography skills and learn all the technicalities that will take them forward in this field.

Eligibility

  • Candidates must have completed their 10+2 or PUC equivalent course with a minimum of 35 percent scores
  • There is no entrance exam to join B.Sc in Photography and the students can join using their 12th class scores.
  • Some universities may have their individual entrance exams and they can be accompanied by personal or group interviews. After they qualify, they are eligible to join this course.

Scope

After completing this course the candidates can get opportunities to work as a wildlife photographer, street photographer, fashion photographer, videographer, still photographer, travel vlogger, photojournalist, travel photographer, sports photographer, etc. They can get employed by travel magazines, magazines, food companies, advertising companies, new channels, newspapers, industries, editing companies, studios, organizations, wildlife and nature companies, etc.
