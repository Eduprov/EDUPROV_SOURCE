Eligibility

These are some of the best courses in designing that are available in the top design colleges of India. This is a 4-year course that has admission applications out for students who have finished 12th or PUC with a minimum of 50% marks. Students from any stream can take up this course. To get admissions into top colleges and top design institutes students must crack competitive national level entrance examinations. Here is a list of entrance exams that lead to admission in the best colleges –

  • CEED (Common Entrance Examination for Design).
  • NID- DAT (National Institute of Design- Design Aptitude Test)
  • UPES- DAT (UPES- Design Aptitude Test)
  • SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test)
  • NIFT Entrance
  • PEARL Entrance Test
  • SOFT Entrance Test
  • NEED (National Entrance Examination for Design)

These entrance exams judge students and rank them based on design aptitude, general aptitude, talent, skill, general knowledge, creativity, spontaneity, planning or approach, abstract thinking, and interpretation skills of students. After passing the first screening test which involves written part, multiple-choice questions, and drawing, painting, and designing questions; students will be selected for a studio test and interviews.
