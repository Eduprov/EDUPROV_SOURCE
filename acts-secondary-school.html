Acts Secondary School

ACTS Secondary School was started in 1998 and is a part of the ACTS Group of Institutions, a registered not-for-profit organization started by Dr. Ken Gnanakan (Ph.D. London) in 1979. ACTS Group is involved in Educational, Environmental, and Health projects around India. ACTS continues to serve in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi, Kolkata, Allahabad, and Kacchawa (UP).
