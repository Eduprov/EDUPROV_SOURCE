Bachelor of Physiotherapy

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) course is a very interesting course with lots of modalities, ways of treatment, application for many diseases and no doubt it covers every nook and corner of the human system. It deals with the science of physical movement and channelizing it to prevent disability and diseases of movement. Physiotherapy or physical therapy is an allied health profession that helps people increase, maintain, restore or retain their physical mobility, function and strength. This science purely makes use of electrotherapy, evidence based kinesiology exercise prescription and shockwave mobility to treat chronic conditions of the bones and soft tissues. Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) is a kind of treatment that is carried out through physical forms such as heat, electricity, mechanical pressure and mechanical forces. Therapy helps to treat disorders without using drugs; physiotherapists have a thorough understanding of the human anatomy and the intricacies of bones, muscles and nerves. BPT course demands its students to have sound knowledge on clinical and scientific rationale behind all the clinical modalities and physical movements of the patient. Scope

Physiotherapists play an important role in the health sector and therefore are required at every hospital and health clinics. There are many job opportunities that a candidate will get after obtaining a degree in physiotherapy as they can join any health and fitness clinic or can start their own private physiotherapy clinic. Also, it is said that many MNC`s hire physiotherapists to ensure that their employees are in good health. After graduation, you can work as a: physiotherapist, sports therapist, acupuncturist, chiropractor, exercise physiologist and others. You can avail employment in different sectors like hospitals, private clinics, special schools, community centers, nursing homes or sports club.

  • A Physiotherapist to a national sporting team.

  • A physiotherapy lecturer in a university
