Jain International Residential School

At JIRS, emphasize on the close integration of online and off-line learning. Computers augment off-line learning but are not substitutes. Students have instant access to information through a well-stocked library and digital hookups with internet and online libraries. Opportunities for creative learning (beyond accessing information) are the touchstone of the school’s IT curriculum.

In today’s global era, eventually, humanities are gaining popularity and they are witnessing a huge number of students who are opting for various programs under humanities. It is a study of different values, cultures, ideas, etc. The main approach of the humanities stream over science and commerce is to solving problems.

After grade 12, students of humanities can take up UG programs with Art, Literature, Education, Law, Civil Services and many more.

Students from JIRS also have the opportunity to take up the Liberal Studies offered by the Jain University which has a wide range of subject combinations of arts, commerce, and science (BA/BSC/BMS)
