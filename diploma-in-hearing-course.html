Diploma in Hearing Language and Speech

Diploma in Hearing Language and Speech Pathology is a two years long course which is specially designed to train the students interested in the study of speech and hearing disorders, its diagnosis, treatment, and care. Joining this course of Diploma in Hearing Language and Speech Pathology will ensure that the students receive training which will help them Identify Communication Disorders, examine it and help the doctors in further diagnosis and treatment. The Diploma in Hearing Language and Speech Pathology Course contains both, theoretical and practical components that will help the students Obtain Knowledge in Language Pathology, Speech pathology, Hearing Aid, Medical Electronics, Audiometry, Hearing Technology, speech disorders, Speech Therapy, Assistive Devices, Anatomy and Physiology of the Human ear, Hearing and Speech Testing, Audiogram, Biochemistry, etc.

Eligibility

  • Admission to Diploma in hearing language and speech pathology is accessible to applicants who have qualified 12th standard / II PUC with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Registrants who might have finished the 10th standard and a diploma in any paramedical field can also join for the diploma in audiology and speech therapy.
  • Candidates should have secured a minimum of 50% of the gross in the qualifying examination. The minimum aggregate score for SC and ST candidates is 45%.
