Diploma in Health Inspector

The Diploma in Health Inspector is a highly lucrative job and made for Students who wish to get a job right after College in the field of pharmacy, especially in the Government sectors After finishing the Course, Students can apply for the post of Health Inspectors, Food Inspectors, drug test centers, government colleges, and private colleges, industries, medical companies, and manufacturing companies, food departments, state inspection authorities, Fassai, etc.

Eligibility

  • The Basic Eligibilities for the students must have passed their 12th standard or 2nd PUC with physics, chemistry and biology subjects or relevant Diploma Degree.
  • They need to score above 45 per cent in their examinations and be over the age of 17 years.

They must apply in the month of June to August when the online applications are open for Students to apply. They must submit their forms online through the KEA portal to get a government seat for Diploma in Health Inspector. The KEA does not conduct any Entrance Exams for Admissions into the Diploma In Health Inspector. It makes a list according to merit order according to the percentage you have in 12th or Diploma.
