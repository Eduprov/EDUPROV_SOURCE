B.Tech Textile Engineering

Textile Engineering is having an increase in demand in the field of engineering. In this course, a candidate learns about the color and fabrics of garments that are used by industries. The basic concept is to study how to yarn, textile fabrics using traditional and modern methods. Textile Industry plays a specific role in growing the economy of the country. Since textile engineering plays a major role in the economy of the country the scope of this course is high. More ideas and details will be provided by the experts at EDUPROV.

B.E/B. Tech Textile Technology is a 4-year degree course. In this course, you will learn about the chemical and physical principles that are used to analyze the polymers of textile fiber.If you opt for this course you will learn about the design and control of textile processes and apparel processes, products, fiber, and machinery.

Scope

After completion of the course, a textile Engineer is liable to work on the post of Technical Services, Quality Control Supervisor, Development Engineers, Operations Trainee, Process Engineer, Technologist, Medical Textile Engineer, Sales Manager, Technical Salesperson and Researcher. There are multinational companies which are expanding their product range, finding the job here is not difficult.

The Textile area is wide and diversified, in the global scale and it has a wide range of job opportunities. Many of international companies are looking for individuals who can work in a significant environment. The availability of the job can be checked through the company's website or one can even do an online job with this course.
