Overview

B.Tech Motorsport Engineering is a very widely chosen field in the engineering discipline. With the advent of new engineering branches also including motorsports it is a relatively new course in India and is being an overly saturated and has a full cutting edge from the competitive market view. More and more students opt this course as the field offers some interesting job opportunities after graduation. With the innovation and adaption of new technologies in the motorsport arena, candidates who are more interested in the automobile and motorsport career can opt this course. However subjective proficiencies of mechanical engineering such as fluid mechanics and mechanical designs which can help the candidate gain some expertise in the course.

Motorsport engineering is a 4 year under graduate program specially introduced to cater to the needs of the motorsport industry. The 4 years are further divided into 6 semesters for 3 years and 1 year compulsory project for successful completion of the course. It is an interdisciplinary approach towards extensive training of professional roles in the motorsport and automobile industry. The course is a multidisciplinary combination of automobile engineering and mechanical engineering. The graduates are eligible to work in any of the above 3 fields after the successful completion of the degree.

The B.Tech Motorsport Engineering course is majorly into designing, developing, innovating and testing new and improvised cars for racing and bikes on tracks. The career opportunities are globally on a hike as they have many opportunities to work in plenty of sectors. They`ll have to carry out multiple jobs in not just one field but in various sectors such as quality control checks before the vehicle goes into full production. Eligibility Criteria

  1. Candidate should have completed their 10+2 from a recognized board.
  2. The candidate must have completed their 12th with Physics, chemistry and mathematics as mandatory subjects.
  3. Students have to score a minimum of 50% in PCM as set for general category and 5% reduction for reserved categories.
  4. Lateral entry process for admissions is also valid.
  5. Students those who have completed their diploma course in motorsport engineering can also apply for this course.

Admission Process

The admission process B.Tech Motorsport Engineering is through entrance exams. Students can choose to write entrance exams as they are conducted on different levels. Entrance exams are conducted state level, national level and a few prestigious universities which give tough competition conduct their own entrance exams known as university level exams. Students depending upon which college they want to go can choose the level of exam they want to attend. There will be seat allotment for students on the basis of the ranking they’ve secured.

The other way of getting admissions is through merit quota. In which the colleges release the merit list on the basis of the students qualifying scores in their previous exam. There are few colleges which offer management seats to students. They can directly apply on the college portal directly and pay the admission fee before the due date. However the seats for management seats will be variably high.

Course Structure
  1. Diploma in Motorsport engineering: Diploma in motorsport engineering is a 3 year course that students can opt after the completion of their 10th or 12th standard depending upon the college requirement.
  2. BSc (Hons) Motorsport Engineering: BSc is in science and the honors course is for a tenure of 3 years which is offered as an online program or a part time program for students to gain extra knowledge. Students can opt this course after the completion of their class 12th.
  3. B.Tech/B.E in Motorsport Engineering: B.E/BTech in motorsport engineering is a 4 year under graduate course in the engineering spectrum which is specially designed for a career in engineering. Students can choose this course after the completion of their 12th standard.
  4. M.Tech/M.E in Motorsport Engineering: MTech/ M.E in Motorsport engineering is a 2 year post graduate course that students can choose after the completion of their undergraduate course. Students choose this course for better career opportunities in future.
