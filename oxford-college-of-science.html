Oxford College Of Science

Oxford College Of Science a college located in Bangalore, Karnataka. It is a part of the Oxford Educational Institutions. The college was established in 1994 with 12 students, within two decades, the college has grown phenomenally with more than 3000 students. It is affiliated to the University of Bangalore, Recognized by UGC, and accredited by International Accreditation Organization (IAO). The science institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Science. It also has an independent placement cell, focuses to cater to the needs of leading organizations in conducting campus interviews for the final year students of M.Sc., MCA, and other UG courses.
