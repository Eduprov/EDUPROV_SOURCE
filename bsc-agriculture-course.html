BSc Agriculture

Course

B.Sc Agriculture or Bachelor of Science is an undergraduate degree awarded by Universities of Agriculture and Agriculture colleges. The course duration is 4 years. It is considered a “Professional” degree by the Government of India. This course is based on the semester system and includes a compulsory one semester of internship (Rural Agricultural Work Experience).

The main purpose of this course is to train students for improving agriculture productivity, manage products, and research activities for future developments. This degree helps one to know about agriculture and the methods and techniques used in the process of farming and related studies.

The agriculture program covers many topics such as Agriculture Science, Use of Scientific Equipment and Techniques in agriculture, Soil Science, land Surveying, Water Resource Management, Animal and Poultry Management, etc.

The simple logic that every human being knows is there is no life without agriculture. In India, the importance of Agriculture is more compared to other sectors. Since our economy is an agrarian economy. The scope of this course does not diminish until we depend on food. If you have your own land as an added advantage, then there is no need to think about it just opt for this course.

Eligibility

The minimum qualification required for B.Sc. Agriculture is 10+2 Science Stream with Physics, Chemistry and Biology subjects or 10+2 in Agriculture stream who have passed their examination with 50% marks from a recognized board.

The information about the institutions that offer this course along with the admission procedure can be obtained from the experts of EDUPROV who are waiting to help you. The highly dedicated professionals will guide you in each and every process and will help you to get into the course.

  • Students can get admission in this course either by Direct Admission or through the written exam conducted by the university/institution.
  • Not all institutions will conduct entrance examinations.

Scope

The demand for trained professionals in Agriculture is very high. India is an agrarian country so students will never run out of job opportunities. Qualified professionals are always valued when they come to this sector. They can get jobs such as Managers at Plantations, Officers, Instructors, Research Assistant, Own agricultural farm, etc. In the Agricultural department, Government banks, Plantations, Fertilizer Manufacturing firms, Agricultural Machinery Industries, Food Processing Units, Academic institutions, etc. Students can get a job in the government sector as well as the private sector. Students can also opt for M. Sc. in the same field.

You can work in various departments like Research Organization, Forest Departments, Academic-Industry, Block Development, and Agricultural Development Offices, Dairy Farm and Pharmaceutical Industries.
