CMR University

CMR University is promoted by CMR Jnanadhara Trust and is established as a private University at Bengaluru through the Karnataka Government by CMR University Act, 2013. CMR University is a New Age University that encourages creativity and cross-disciplinary learning through its unique ‘Education Engagement Model’. With an industry-designed and developed curriculum of study, the focus is on flexible, experiential and integrated learning, involving a blend of intensive academic interventions such as group discussions, case studies, participatory classroom approach, choice of electives and more. They believe in creating knowledge partnerships with industry to plug the industry-academia gap and deliver the latest skills. CMR's core strength lies in its faculty body, a balanced blend of rich expertise from academic, industry, consultancy and research.

Admission Procedure

  • Original marks card of SSLC/10Std/intermediate for, age proof, 10+2/PUC/equivalent, Original transfer certificates, Original migration certificate (for other than the Karnataka State Board students).
  • 12 recent passport size photographs.
  • Admission will be based on the performance in KCET/ COMEDK.

It is to be noted that qualified foreign nationals are provisionally admitted subject to regularization after obtaining the eligibility certificate issued by the Bangalore university. Student visa, Residential permit, and serology report (HIV Test), AIU Letter for foreign nationals are a must.
