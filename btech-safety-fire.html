B.Tech Fire and Safety Engineering

B.Tech Fire and Safety Engineering is a very renowned course in Industrial engineering. The course is industry-based course in the engineering arena. This course of fire and safety engineering is designed to create awareness and gives the power to techniques to create safety against fire.

B.Tech Fire and Safety Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate degree which is further divided into 8 semesters. Students are taught detailed study on subjects like hydraulics, fire communications, fire rescue, salvage investigation, disaster mitigation and town planning.

B.Tech Fire and Safety Engineering is mainly focused on designing and development of safer fire equipment’s, fire resistance machineries and plant as well as in-depth information on fire, understanding fire and its hazardous events, control measures etc. This course is very apt for those students who are looking out specifically for a career in Industrial engineering with fire and safety engineering. They usually get employed in public and private sector organizations specially in government constructing firms. The course is also suitable for those students who are daring to fight against the furious natural resource like fire.

In order to take up Fire and Safety Engineering course, students need to have good communication skills, decision making abilities, evaluation in difficult situations, problem solving techniques. The other basic qualities that are required in a candidate to be physically fit, with presence of mind and self-disciplined enough with good leadership qualities.

Scope and Career

The career prospect has an evaluating scheme towards opportunities. The course has gained huge popularity and immense knowledge in just a few years. This course is on trending list and one of the prime factors responsible for this are the ample job opportunities for individuals for fire and safety professionals. The course includes both theoretical and practical knowledge along with practical lab sessions which trains the students on how to act and react alongside the usage of technology in relevance to safety.

Government has ensured the emphasis of workplace safety, and every individual life matters more. Thus, to ensure the safety of personnel at work place they require professional and skilled safety and fire engineers. There are many industries where the skills and abilities of a fire and safety engineer would come into picture, such as chemical factories, fire cracker manufacturing units, gas plants, refineries can never be safe and devoid of danger. This is where fire and safety engineers come into picture.

After the completion of the 4 year under graduate degree course, students can take up their higher education such as MTech or M.E. in Fire and Safety Engineering which will turn them into skilled fire and safety professionals. They are mainly responsible to undertake safety precautions in any kind of fire accidents. They are also into developing and discovering new innovative technologies in fire detectors, fire preventions, air sampling, UV flame detectors etc.

Those who are into invention and innovation in technology will take up PhD and work in research and development. The salary trends for B.Tech Fire and Safety Engineering are very good and has given ample career opportunities to young candidates for a better future.
