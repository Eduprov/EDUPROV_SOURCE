B.Tech Dairy Technology

B.Tech Dairy Technology as the name suggest the course purely deals with milk processing and related products. Unlike other courses this course is not much popular and are not available in every college. But the scope of this course is unbelievable with good income.

One who opts for this course will know about the process involves the latest technological equipment to process dairy products. This includes the process in which the toxins are removed without touching the nutrient factors of the milk.

Eligibility

  • To do a degree in Dairy Technology and Engineering one should either have a diploma in certain courses or they should have completed 12th.

To avail in diploma course, a person has to complete the 10th exam. The diploma programs to choose from is a Diploma in Food and Dairy Technology, Diploma in Dairy Technology and Advanced Diploma in Dairy Science and Technology.

If a person holds a diploma degree in the above-mentioned courses they are eligible for doing Dairy Technology and Engineering they can get into the second year directly. In the case of +2, the minimum requirement to pursue this course is to pass 10+2 with Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics. The passing marks in the 12th should be above 50% and also it is necessary to pass an entrance Exam. The guidance for entrance exams about the syllabus and how to prepare for the exam will be provided by the experts of EDUPROV.

Master’s Degree: For attaining a Master’s Degree in the field of Dairy Technology and engineer it is necessary to pass the Bachelor’s course. An aspirant is required to take the GATE Exam or qualify the college entrance exam. The last step to get avail for the Master’s degree is going through a Personal Interview.

Scope

After gaining the degree a candidate can start their own dairy business on a small scale. This business will be related to dairy products like creamery, ice-cream, storage, and packaging units and milk plants. A degree holder is also eligible to work in the job profile of the Commodity Trading Manager, Head Quality Control, Industry Supervisor, Dairy Scientist, Nutritionists, Dairy Technologist, Product development Manager, Market Development Manager, Dairy Medical Officer, Theologians, Nutritionists, Dairy Technologist.

The one who completed this degree is much wanted in Multinational Companies. One of the few ways to join them is to start working as a researcher in milk products.
