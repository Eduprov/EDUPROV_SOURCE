Bachelor in Medical Records Science B.M.R.Sc

With the advancement of medical science since 2000, a lot of segmental branches have cropped up to simplify the trade. One of the vital divisions of medical science is the medical record science. This course is suitable for the one who loves to do research and can dedicate themselves in the lab for hours with the team spirit.

Bachelor of medical record science is a three-year bachelor’s program< that deals with various aspects of record maintenance and storage along with mass data handling regarding a variety of drugs, diseases, and organisms. At the same time, students of medical record science are taught to deal with available systems of scientific record maintenance. It includes billing and usage of medical software. As a matter of fact, medical record science is a vast course that contributes to the advancement in scientific data and its analysis.

Eligibility

  • You need to have a minimum qualification of 10+2 with physics, chemistry, and biology to pursue B.M.R.Sc in any government or private college. Mathematics is not compulsory.
  • You need to secure minimum 45-50% to get admission to this course. To be specific, the eligibility criteria for MBBS is followed in this course.

Scope

The overall scope of this course can be subdivided into two leading categories:

Academic: students can pursue higher education in various fields involving medical and business trades. Some of the higher education options include post-graduation in medical record science, Bioinformatics, all other branches related to biology and medical science. Apart from that, students of B.M.R.Sc have also excelled in the field of forensic science and data analysis.

Professional field: usually the students holding a degree on B.M.R.Sc can take up various jobs that include data analysis, collection, storage and maintenance in various health centers and medical institutions. The increasing amount of diagnostic centers and healthcare have opened diverse opportunities for B.M.R.Sc degree holders.

Generally, a large number of healthcare institutions and medicals require experts to collect, segregate data related to various symptoms, diseases, and cures. And usually, these data are not effectively managed by general accountants or just anybody. For the specific needs, B.M.R.Sc degree holders are hired by these institutions.
