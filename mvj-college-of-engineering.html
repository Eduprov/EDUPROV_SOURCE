MVJ College Of Engineering (MVJCE)

MVJ College Of Engineering (MVJCE), Bangalore is ranked 52 for Electrical Engineering by India Today and among Top 4 colleges in VTU for academic excellence. The institute is best known for its M.Tech and MBA program. More than 200 companies have visited the institute for on-campus placement and a total of 750+ jobs are offered every year.

MVJCE Bangalore comes under the umbrella of MVJ Group of Institutions. It offers degree programs in the discipline of Engineering and Business Admissions are done on the basis of merit and the cutoff criteria of the entrance exams accepted by the institute.
