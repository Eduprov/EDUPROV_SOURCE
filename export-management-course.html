MBA Export Management

Course

Export management majorly focuses on the garment industry. The course covers various areas in export management, such as production, making, marketing, managing, designing, and procurement. This course is quite popular in garment manufacturing areas.

Eligibility

Diplomas, Undergraduate degree, Certificate, Postgraduate degree, PG Diploma are the different types of courses available in order to obtain knowledge in the Export management sector.

BBA (Export Management) is a degree level course and the course duration is commonly 3 years. There are many institutions in India that are offering this course. The candidates who have passed their 10+2 examination or the equivalent examination is eligible to enter into this course. One can also go for part-time as well as distance learning for this course. The course syllabus is generally divided into 6 semesters and if passed with good marks one can enjoy a number of opportunities in the job sector.

Bachelor of Arts in Export management is also a degree level course. The candidates who have passed their 10+2 examination or the equivalent examination in the Arts stream, most of the institutions offering this course conduct an entrance examination, which the candidate has to crack with good marks. There is a certain age criteria for the full time course, but if a candidate opts for part time or distance learning course then there is no age limit.

Master of Business Administration (Export Management) is a post graduate degree with the course duration of two years. All candidates who have successfully completed their graduation degree are eligible for this course. Entrance examinations like CAT, GATE, and GMAT are the common gateway for this course in reputed institutions.

Diploma and Certification are available both in UG and Advanced level, which is usually of one year. One who needs some added advantage in their career and loves to gain knowledge in a short period can opt for this course. UG degree is the eligibility criteria in most organizations.

Scope

Bachelor of Arts in Export management is suitable for candidates who are interested in the field of fashion, designing and textile. Those who are interested in teaching will have to apply for the post graduation level course. Hard work and knowledge can offer you huge opportunities of a successful career. A candidate may get a job in fashion designing companies as an export management professional. The scope of this course is equally high in the nation and overseas.
