BNM Institute of Technology

B.N.M. Institute of Technology is a private technical co-educational school located in Bangalore, Karnataka. The institute was established in the year 2001 under Bhageerathi Bai Narayana Rao Maanay Charity Trust. BNMIT has been accredited with ‘A’ grade with NAAC. BNMIT is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum. Admission to BNMIT will be on the basis of National level exams such as KCET, COMEDK, CAT, MAT etc.

The Institute offers B.E., M.Tech and MBA program in several specializations at the Undergraduate and Postgraduate level. Additionally, the institute also offers a Ph.D. program to the candidates at the Doctoral level.
