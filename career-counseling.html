Career Counseling

Career counseling one of the most prominent function of EDUPROV Educational Institution. EDUPROV has been excelling in career counseling over a decade now and has highly made changes not just in the ways of convincing students to choose what they want but has also evolved more in academic reformations and revolutions. Career counseling is an act or process of helping students to understand and know the macrocosm of work in order to make a career, good enough to consider your educational and life decisions. Career counseling is a very smooth process which has two entities; the counselor and the counselee.

Career guidance helps you to understand and fathom what job you want to get in future after you graduate. It is a life changing process as you will be guided towards picking a right course, which will eventually lead you to pick a right job which will be highly beneficial for you. Picking the right course and the right job is very pivotal as you grow up, you will change, your life will transform, situations will mutate, and this crisis it is required that you choose the right career options. In this process you will continually have to keep making decisions throughout your life. The goal of career counseling is not just to help you make decisions but it helps you give knowledge and skills that you might need in order to make future decisions.

Many students who have just completed their 10th and 12th standard definitely need some chunk of advice so that they don’t land themselves into the wrong course. Students right after their completion have a plethora of choices to deal from. They`ve been offered courses in three main streams like science, arts, humanities and commerce. They’ve many other courses like Diploma, short term certificate courses, vocational and non- vocational courses, ITI courses.

Choosing one amongst all these options is like picking the outstanding dress to look best among all. Usually students pick the wrong choices. Few common challenges faced by students while picking a course :

  • Family pressure
  • Parents expectations
  • Societal barriers
  • Passion v/s skill
  • Salary driven decisions
  • Poor self confidence
  • Self esteem
  • Tough competition
  • Various career opportunities that could baffle the mind of youngsters.

There are many things that students need to take into consideration if they want to build a strong career setup. Evaluation of skills, aptitude, personality, and interest are a few components which a student needs to look into in order to choose a job. The first and foremost step in career guidance is picking the right course. There is a pyramid that which you need to follow if you want a better career growth.

We at EDUPROV help the students in necessitating their job requirements by keenly paying heed to the students perception of achieving goals. Right course+ Right College+ Right Placement = Best Career.

EDUPROV`S Approach Towards Students for Career Counseling

  1. Career counselors at EDUPROV will help you figure out who you are, and what exactly are you looking out from life. What exactly you want from your career and your education.
  2. We at EDUPROV helps the students to identify and analyze their inner personality through SWOT Analysis: S - Strengths, W- Weaknesses, O- Opportunities, T- Threats.
  3. Through career counseling we ensure that we help the students open up to the counselor freely. Students can talk about their thoughts, opinions, ideas, feelings, concerns and any obstacles if any. By analyzing this the counselors will help you to sort out, organize and plan things accordingly.
  4. Counselors at EDUPROV guide the students to determine the next step and foster a plan of action to achieve those goals.
  5. Career counselors at EDUPROV are skilled and have high expertise in their respective arena.
  6. Through career counseling we help the students discover their innate abilities to do a job.
  7. Career counselors at EDUPROV maintain high confidentiality about the counselee. This enhances trust between a counselor and the counselee.
  8. Career counselors at EDUPROV don’t just give suggestions but work hand on hand with the students by helping them research on the right job, find resources for strengthening skills needed for the job.

Career counseling is a very intimate professions. So at EDUPROV our counselors are socializing, helping others and teaching new skills to improvise their job arena.
