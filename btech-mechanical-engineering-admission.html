B.Tech Mechanical Engineering

B.Tech Mechanical Engineering is one amongst the most preferred courses in the engineering streamlet. As we all know that engineering has various streams and a lot of courses that are high on demand and has opened a lot of gateways into the best career for students Get B.Tech Mechanical Engineering Admission in Bangalore at Eduprov.

B.Tech Mechanical Engineering is a 4 year undergraduate engineering course which specifies or gives a brief study on production, designing and operation of machines. Students who are more interested and have a liking towards applications of machines, their functions and different principles can opt for this course as it also gives the student a top end source over the best career options.

Mechanical Engineering is one of the most diversified and versatile engineering branch which plays a pivotal role in human life. In general a mechanical engineer helps in developing products by coming up with new ideas, performing analysis on different structures, coming up with strategies, manufacturing technologies and production process, analysis and tracking the machine running process and performs the intended functions.

The main objective behind taking up Mechanical Engineering is to prepare the students to apply the modules and principles of engineering on machines, for the designing, manufacturing and maintenance of mechanical systems and structures.

Eligibility criteria

  • The candidate should have completed their 10+2 from a recognized board.
  • Earlier, students had to have a minimum of 45% in the qualifying examination to be eligible to apply.
  • Candidates who passed Class 12 or PUC or equivalent eligible to apply for the KCET 2021.
  • They should have studied Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics as mandatory subjects.
  • Students with a 3-year Diploma in the related field are eligible to apply for B. Tech Lateral Entry Admissions.
