Overview

United Kingdom popularly named as Britain is a country located off the northwest coast whose capital city is London, which is known for the leading commercial trade and cultural centre. As UK is the home of English language there are students who consider choosing the country as their study abroad location. There are students from all across the globe who come to study in the UK is because of its vibrant and ingenious culture. The standard of living is very lofty and introduces the student to a different philosophy and a well-built mind-set. Students who study in UK come across people from varied nationalities with metalinguistic backgrounds.

Universities and colleges in UK have been consistently ranked as the best worldwide is because they are globally recognized and valued. The quality of education is exceptional and the universities have maintained a standardized culture to uphold the teaching calibre which has made the institution an excellence for the same. The central body which is responsible to maintain spontaneity in these standards is The Quality Assurance Agency for higher education. Another reason for students to choose UK after their 10th, 12th or graduation is because of the expansive course options with distinctive specializations that encourages students to surpass their reassessment over study abroad locations.

Education System in United Kingdom

The education system in the U.K. (except for Scotland) comprises four main sectors: primary, secondary, further education, and higher education. The U.K. ranks second to the U.S. in international students’ preferences for study destinations. Since the 1999 launch of the Prime Minister’s Initiative (PMI), the U.K. has focused on providing more international student places in further and higher education. The U.K. has numerous further and higher education institutions for the international student to consider, and English-language courses are readily available throughout the four countries. The UK has just over 450,000 international students enrolled in higher education.

While the four countries of the U.K. have differing approaches to vocational education and training (VET), the training and qualifications are interchangeable and of the same standard, three of the countries (England, Wales, and Northern Ireland) share a common system of external qualifications within the National Qualifications Framework.

Education in Scotland

The Scottish system of education is quite distinct from the education systems of the rest of the U.K. From 1999, it has had its own legislative framework, curriculum framework, and qualifications system. Universities are located in all of the major cities of the country. Higher education courses in Scotland are usually one year longer than in other countries of the U.K. Emphasis is placed on breadth in a wide range of specialised subjects. Scotland prides itself on the standard of education provided and flexibility allowed in selecting subjects within a course.

Entrance Exams

The UK is a very sought-after foreign choice for a student`s education with almost 18 universities in world`s top 100 rankings. But getting until UK with its pristine quality education and a culture rich in world-class qualification you need to write a standardized test as per the university requirement.

  1. IELTS UKVI: International English Language Testing system is a very known and attempted Language proficiency test. The average score requirement in many universities would be 7.0 considering which college or university they choose.
  2. TOEFL: Test of English as a foreign language is a test taken by many students ahead of their admissions. But owing to many fraudulent activities UK British Home office no longer accepts TOEFL exams as a part of the visa application.
  3. PTE: Pearson Test English UK is highly preferred since 98 universities of UK have recognized it which comprises of reading, writing, hearing and speaking. 51 is the average score requirement with 65-75 for regular university selections.
  1. GMAT: The GMAT is an admission test for under graduation in management. The candidates planning to do business management degrees can write GMAT and is a recognized test worldwide which analyses the general understanding of algebra, arithmetic, geometry and grammar.
  2. GRE FOR UK: GMAT and GRE are primarily distinctive in nature while many candidates consider it to be similar. GRE is a standardized examination for candidates who are graduates at UK institutions. GMAT is for students who want to choose business degree while GRE is necessary regardless of your graduate program. The GRE is divided in 3 sections such as analytical, quantitative and verbal reasoning.
  3. LNAT UK: The LNAT UK is for those candidates who want to pursue their law studies, these analyses if the candidate is seeking for a university sea has certain qualities such as verbal reasoning, information, interpretation and problem-solving techniques.
  4. MAT Mathematics Admissions Test: Oxford and Cambridge two of UK`s most prominent institutions jointly conduct this Math examination which evaluates the candidates in certain mathematic field, this exam is both for students taking up Bachelor of Math or CS program.
  1. GAMSAT Exam: This exam is also known as Graduate Medical School Admission test is for those candidates who wish to pursue their PhD program in Australia, UK or Ireland. The finest applicants will be taken by the Consortium of Graduate Medical schools.
  2. BMAT Exam: Any candidate who is looking out to pursue medical, biomedical and dentistry program need to take up the BMAT UK exam.
  3. UK CAT: UK CAT Clinical Aptitude Test for medical and dentistry students to be effective and efficient enough to perform any activities related to the same, universities in UK conduct the UK CAT exam.
