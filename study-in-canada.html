Overview

Canada is considered to be the best study destination for students across the globe with high academic excellence and affordable tuition fee. Canada being a big country which stretches from east to west almost 4700 miles and is considered to be the second largest country in the world. There are many students who want to pursue their higher education or post- graduation in Canada considering the life and education system which has encouraged many students to make the country as their preferential choice. Canada is much known for its professionalism in teaching and its well renowned universities which offer a brawny career with immense career opportunities.

Now, before choosing any university a student will also consider the finance factor. It hits the priority of the student as you need to understand the feasibility of looking for the course. There will be many factors such as food, accommodation, health coverage, travelling expenses, hostel accommodation and tuition fee. Study abroad in Canada can be very expensive, is a myth. There are many universities and colleges in Canada which offer scholarships and educational loans to merit students who face difficulties in funding their education and fulfil the expenses. The amount of money conceded varies from university to university and students need to apply 8-12 months in prior to the admissions. The country also offers vocational schools and apprenticeship trainings for those students who want to improvise their skills in trade by gearing up with the advanced technology.

Canadian education is considered to be highbred because of its wide range of world class eminent universities and colleges and also counted as the third best country in the world for education. Also, the other main reason as to why many students choose Canada as their option is because it offers diverse range of courses along with multiple specialization. Canada offers the students MBA course, Medical courses, Arts courses, Law, Management and BSc courses, technical courses, commerce courses and plenty of vocational courses and apprenticeship trainings. The colleges are tied up with top companies and give 100% placements to students which attracts students from across the globe. The vast majority of programs has encouraged more than half a million students from other countries as well to choose Canada.

Why to Study in Canada?

Canada can be the Ideal country as to why you’d want to finish your UG or PG either it could be MBA, Medical, MS or PhD. Here are a few reasons as to why you need to be choosing Canada.

  1. Home to World renowned Universities: Canada is a beautiful country which is hosting the top esteemed universities and institutions such as University of Toronto, Durham College in Oshawa, University of Alberta, University of Montreal, Mc Master University of Hamilton etc.
  2. Affordable fee and study expenses: Canada is one such university which is pretty affordable in terms of fee, accommodation and more compared to the other countries such as UK and USA.
  3. Standardized living: The rich standard of living and quality effective method of teaching is the main reason why many students choose to study in Canada. The enriched lifestyle helps students interact with different cultures and students from all across the world.
  4. Top class education system: Canada is well known for its education system which encourages students to take up academics in a very innovative and strategic manner. It considers the students opinions, thoughts, and gives liberty to express their ideas. The education system evaluates the student’s overall performance of athletics, sports, extra- curricular activities and academics altogether for the comprehensive development of the student.
  5. Refined career opportunities: With high number of graduates and post graduates, Canada offers students good and refined career opportunities in a cosmopolitan environment to enhance and build connections in the global hub. The colleges and universities are very well known for their top placement cells placing graduates in good numbers.

Prerequisites to get Admission in Canada

There are different universities which have their own demands and expectations but there are a few common requirements in every university which the student should be well aware of either the admission prerequisites and the university requirements.

  1. Fill up the application form of the chosen university.
  2. Official transcripts along with documentation of the former degree.
  3. 2 letters of academic reference attesting the purpose of study and readiness for graduate studies.
  4. A detailed sequential resume, stating the academic requirements, educational achievements, work experience and other data.
  5. A letter of Intent (LOI) which explains why the candidate is applying and for what purpose.
  6. An accreditation from a recognized institute confirming the applicant`s documents from a verified institute by the admission committee.
  7. Proof of English language.

Which English Language Tests Are Required to Study in Canada?

  1. International English Language Testing System (IELTS) Academic: IELTS, an internationally-owned and highest quality English language test offered. The score for study Canada by many universities acceptable is 7.0 but many universities take 5.0 to 7.5.
  2. Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) Academic: English foreign language test works as a significant test which evaluates the student’s capacity to study or work. TOEFL score accepted by many Canadian universities is 580 or <90 on 100.
  3. Pearson Test of English (PTE) - Academic: PTE Pearson Test of English is recognized by many universities and colleges on Canada. The PTE falls in a range of 10-90 which is adequately considered as 50-63, but for higher and regular universities it falls to 65-75.
  4. Canadian Academic English Language (CAEL) assessment; The testing service is available to international students from Brazil, China, Colombia, India, Japan, Mexico, UAE, the USA, and the Philippines.

Entrance exams

  1. GMAT Graduate Management Aptitude Test (GMAT) Canada: GMAT is peculiarly for those students who want to do their MBA. This exam enhances the skills of students those who want to pursue their business or management studies. There are certain universities which accept GMAT scores and certain universities in Canada give admissions for MBA without GMAT.
  2. GRE Graduate Record Examination (GRE) Canada: If candidates want to choose studying their MA or MS in Canada can plan to write GRE. But many universities don’t take up GRE scores except for a few such as masters or online program.
  3. Medical college Admission Test (MCAT) Canada:
  4. For Medical aspirants who want to pursue MS or MD or any medical courses and this exam analyses the critical, functional and biological sciences with the ability to reason out things. Top universities of medical do require the best scores in MCAT exam.
  5. LSAT Law school admission test (LSAT) Canada: LSAT is a very standard examination which admits students in top law colleges of Canada. If you apply for Canadian Law school then a standardized test is to be written to evaluate the student’s abilities for a logical reasoning.
  6. Scholastic Assessment Test (SAT) Canada: SAT exam is for students seeking admission to undergraduate schools. SAT test has been developed to evaluate the written, verbal and mathematical skills of the candidates.
