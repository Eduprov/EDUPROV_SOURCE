Don Bosco Institute of Technology

Don Bosco Institute of Technology was established in 2001 and has become one of the leading technology institutes in Bangalore. The college has successfully build an atmosphere attributing to the research activities and is one of the leading institutes providing quality technological and managerial education.

The college has been affiliated to Visweshwaraya Technological University (VTU) and all the courses are approved by AICTE.

The college works with a mission to create technocrats and leaders who can brighten up the future of the country and to provide an environment to foster intellectual and ethical growth of the students.

The college has excellent infrastructure, with library, laboratories, spacious class rooms, and various other facilities to bring out the best of a students’ creativeness.

The college provides 100% placement facility to its students and the top most recruiting firms from the college include: Wipro, Accenture, ICICI Bank, Tata Elxsi, L&T Infotech etc.

DBIT has been awarded as “Best & Most promising Engineering College in Karnataka”. The college offers both UG as well as PG courses in Engineering as well as Management course.

With the aim of producing leaders of tomorrow, the college aims at providing best education with its very dedicated faculty. The college has many departments offering both undergraduate courses as well as postgraduate courses. The courses offered by the college are as follows:

The students here take an active part in extra-curricular activities besides concentrating on academic excellence. The various activities and departments promoting extra-curricular activities in the college are as follows:

The library has been named as Dr. Raja Ramanna Center for Knowledge Resources and is the heart of all the academic activities. The library has been spread over an area of 800 sq.m. and is fully comprehensive, has a vast resource of books, journals etc. of all the courses be it engineering or management. The number of books library has is approximately 37070 and houses a number of project reports, E-books, E-journals, VTU question papers etc.

The campus has a separate residential facility for both boys and girls. The hostels are provided with various recreational activities like reading rooms, Wi-Fi facility, celebrating festivals and various facilities of daily use like solar heaters, cooking accessories etc.

The campus also has mess facility which provides food to the hostellers and caters to their needs and nutritional values as well. Non vegetarian food is also served once a week and food quality is very good.

The college believes in making their students both mentally as well as physically active thus they provide gymnasium and jogging track facility for their physical fitness.

The students and faculty members are provided with broadband as well as Wi-Fi facility and the library also has internet facility for accessing e-books and e-journals.

The campus has association with Axis bank and ATM facility is also provided in the campus.

The college has a lush green campus with beautiful gardens to relax and recreate. The annual cultural as well as technical festival of the college is VISMAY.
