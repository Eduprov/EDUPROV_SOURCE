Overview

Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Electrical Engineering / B.Tech in Electronics and Electrical Engineering / B.Tech in EEE is a major course under the engineering orbit. The B.Tech EEE program is a 4 year under graduate program which is a technically skilled index widely opted by many students all over the world, which is a mixed combination of electronics and electrical knowledge. B.Tech in Electronics and Electrical Engineering has widespread knowledge on electronics and electrical components and deals with areas like designing and maintaining of electrical machines, electrical appliances, electrical sockets, circuit design, vehicle electrical manufacturing, electronic device manufacturing, automation design, power house maintenance, Hydro electric plant and much more concerned with electrical and electronics.

B.Tech EEE deals with the standardization in the technological and electrical aspects in a computerized format. As the world getting more and more innovated thus the need to understand the circuits and technologically skilled electrical devices knowledge should be escalated. EEE involves the use of power in all its components. Power can be involved in turbines hydro, gas, fuel, bio solar, wind solar, or geo thermal energy and to run wind mills. As the name is Electronics and Electrical but also involves study in mechanical, computer and communication field.

With B.Tech EEE course students who are more inclined towards digital and analog electronics and circuits, their components microprocessors with communication with signal processing alongside embedded system can choose this course which will enhance their skills as they can passionately do what they love.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. The candidate must have finished their 10+2 from a recognized board.
  2. The candidate must have completed their 12th with Physics, Chemistry, Math, Computers, or Electronics as mandatory subjects.
  3. The candidate must have scored 45% as aggregate for General Category, for other categories like SC/ST an aggregate of 40% must have been secured.

Admission Process

The admission process in B.Tech EEE the student must fulfill the above stated eligibility criteria. In order to get admissions in B.Tech EEE program students need to appear in the entrance exams and get a qualifying rank to get a cost effective admission process. Some of the few popular entrance exams are:

  1. KCET
  2. JEE Mains
  3. JEE Advanced
  4. COMED K

Students need to attend any of these entrance exams in order to get admissions in top colleges. Entrance exams act as a gateway for students which requires them to attend the exam so that they prove their eligibility for the course. There will be a cut off percentage and the students need to score a minimum near to the cut off and if they`re scoring more than the cut off the fees can be less and they will have added benefits if they score in merit and can choose amongst a list of best colleges.

Now there are certain other eminent universities which conduct their own entrance exams. Those can be called as university level entrance exams. As the universities are approved and desired by many students and the colleges will be running high on demand so as the level of competition.

Students those who are planning to do engineering can apply for JEE Mains right after their 12th examination. For students who want to get admissions into the prestigious universities like NIT`s IIT`s need to qualify JEE Mains along with JEE Advanced to get a seat as the stakes of competition are quiet high. Course Structure

Diploma in EEE: Diploma is for a duration of 3 years and candidates can choose this course right after completion of 10th standard. They need have to do their 12th as it is not considered to be mandatory for getting admissions in Diploma.

Graduation in EEE: B.Tech or BE in EEE is a 4 year Under Graduate course which a student can choose only after completion of 12th from a recognized board with Physics, chemistry and Math or Electronics as compulsory subjects.

Post-Graduation in EEE: Post- Graduation in EEE is course with a 2 year duration also known as Masters or M.Tech in EEE. It has a high scope and offers brilliant career opportunities once completion of degree.

Career and Scope

After completion of B.Tech in Electricals and Electronics Engineering the student has a wide arena of job placements to choose from. The field electronics and electrical engineering is vast and candidates can build a successful career if once the field chosen matches their skills. This field opens up various career prospects for students in the field of telecom industries, IT industries, manufacturing and telecommunication industries, production and distribution of power companies etc. Candidates cannot only work in technical field but those who are interested in building a career in teaching can also work as lecturers and join prestigious institutions and can do some more research about EEE can pursue PH. D or doctorate degree in order to have career growth.

The scope of B.Tech EEE is high both in India and abroad as it is relatively new field and this field verily influences our life and has increased our standard of living as well as cut down on man power as electrical devices are much in use now. This field helps develop, design, innovate and renovate the current technology and finds ways to improvise the coming and new technology. As the demand for this course has increased, more demand for highly skilled electrical and electronic engineers has also risen efficiently. These opportunities are not just limited to private sector but spread vastly even to the government sectors.
