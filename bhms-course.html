Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medical and surgery

BHMS or Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgeryis an excellent undergraduate degree in the medical field that covers the medical knowledge from the homeopathic medical system to train you in the system to become a homeopathic doctor after the completion of your degree at the undergraduate level. It is one of the three most popular coursesamong students who wish to become doctors along with the MBBS or allopathic and Ayurveda courses. As it is well known, Homeopathy is the holistic medical system that is involved in the treatment of patients with the high dilutions of homeopathic medicines mainly in liquid and tablet form, which enhance the body’s natural healing system.

Bachelor of homeopathic medicine and surgery or B.H.M.S is a five and a half year-long undergraduate degree that students can join as soon as they complete their 12th or PUC for getting a high school level education. The first four and a half years of the undergraduate degree is mainly divided intoclassroom lectures, practical lab sessions, and hospital rounds and doctor shadowing to learn about the system, treating patients, and the techniques a homeopathic doctor should know when he or she is treating patients.

Eligibility

  • The students seeking admissions must be at least 17 years old.
  • The candidates must have passed their 10+2 education exams with at least 50 percent to qualify for admissions into the BHMS course at the undergraduate level.
  • The applicants must have had physics, chemistry, and biology as their main or core subjects in their 12th or PUC level.

The selection of candidates shall be finalized only on the basis of their final merit as that can be decided based on the total marks and percentage obtained in 12th or high school level, the performance of the student in the National Entrance Examination Test (NEET) and the personal interview if required by the college during admissions or selections.

Some of the most popular course specializations chosen by students are in the field of homeopathic pharmacy, pediatrics, psychiatry, infertility, skin specialty, and immunology. Besides the Bachelor’s degree in homeopathy, there are some more courses in the field of homeopathy medicine so that you can take a path towards becoming a homeopathy doctor in your career
