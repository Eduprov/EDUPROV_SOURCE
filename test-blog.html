B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering

B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering is a professional course in the engineering streamlet which deals with the principles of an aircraft, spacecraft, its modules, variants, their technicalities and their flying abilities. Aeronautical Engineering teaches the design and development of an aircraft/ spacecraft. It studies about the physical properties behind a space shuttle, a rocket, flying planes, the science which is being implemented in order to fly all these huge objects, the aerodynamics structure and their physical aspects. This particular stream of engineering deals with the modern and recent adaptations of various analysis on these huge objects and comes up with strategies to be confined to not just the old modules but to develop a better and enhanced structure for a more effective future of flying planes Get B.Tech Aeronautical Engineering Admission in Bangalore at Eduprov.

Students who love to design aircrafts and space crafts those who have an inclination towards aviation industry are entitled to enormous job opportunities in this blooming field.

B Tech in Aeronautical Engineering is a 4 year Under graduate course which teaches about the aviation techniques, systems, and designs the control and operation, the functioning and controlling of aircrafts and space crafts. This major field of engineering focuses on the development and designing of huge flying models.

Eligibility criteria

  • The candidate should have completed their 10 + 2 | PUC from a recognized board.
  • They should have studied Physics, Chemistry and Math as compulsory subjects.
  • Earlier, students had to have a aggregate of 45% in their Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics examination to be eligible to apply.
  • Candidates who passed Class 12 or PUC or equivalent eligible to apply for the KCET 2021.

Admission process

One way of getting admissions for Aeronautical Engineering is through entrance test. Either national level entrance exams, or state level entrance exams. Also there are certain institutions which conduct their own entrance exams. Students can opt for such colleges or universities as well. These are all private institutions and also are well reputed. The procedure is basically like it holds entrance test and they set a particular cut off percentile. The students just has to qualify and get admissions followed by a counseling session.
