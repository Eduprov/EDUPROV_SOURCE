B.Tech Information Technology

The change that we see in our world today is because of the development of this technology (i.e) Information Technology. The computers have reached houses and there are people who are using them every day. The person who chooses IT will know about computer software and the hardware. The demand for IT professionals< is increasing day today. Choosing Information Technology is a wise choice as the demand for it won't diminish easily.

B.Tech Information Technology or IT is an undergraduate degree in the field of computer hardware and software. The course deals with the concept of software programmers, network administrators, computer scientists, and system analysts. Within the course, a candidate learns all the major concepts of networks, software applications, and hardware components.

IT professionals are always in demand from various companies within or outside the country. An IT professional's work is to bring a new concept or deal with older concepts while correcting the problems.

Eligibility

  • B.Tech IT: The course has also termed a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology.
  • To get avail in the course a candidate should pass the 10+2 examination with Chemistry, Physics, and Math.

A student has to study for 4 years to complete this program. Mainly the colleges ask the student to get qualified from AIEEE (All India Engineering Entrance Exam) or any other common entrance exam for Engineering. Some of the colleges are having their own course of Entrance Exam. The duration of completion of this program is 4 years.

Scope

After successful completion of the course a person can start working as a Software Developer, Network Engineer and Administrator, Computer Scientist or work in any other computer field. The demand for an IT is always there and most companies are hiring than with a nice salary package. As the aspirant is studying there are companies from international brands that come for campus selection. This is one of the easiest ways of getting selected in a foreign company. On the other hand, there are vacancies present online.
