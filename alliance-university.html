Alliance University

Alliance University Bangalore has been accorded recoganisations across the globe from various reputed organizations – #7 (Best Private University 2018) by India Today, #10 (Best B-School in India offering Global Business Courses 2018) by Outlook and many more. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral and professional courses in Engineering, Management, Commerce and Law streams.

This institute also provides the place for students to meet and interact with each other in a comfortable environment through alumni meet conducted by the alumni committee. The campus of this institution gives the nature’s touch like it has beautiful gardens, flower beds, fruits and flower bearing trees, water ponds, park furniture, etc.

  • Fill in the application form.
  • Duly filled form should be submitted along with two passport size photographs.
  • Fees payment can be done either by cash/ cheque on the same date.
  • This confirms the enrollment procedure. Students are personally informed about the date of commencement of their course.
