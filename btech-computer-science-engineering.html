B.Tech Computer Science Engineering

B.Tech Computer Science Engineering is one of the most hunted courses in engineering profession. It takes a huge lead in the education world as the real world applications of the computer engineering course haven grown augmented over the past decade, which is why there has been a steady increase in the demand for well- trained computer science graduates. Is there anyone out there who loves programming, coding, embedding, micro blogging, web designing and much more, Then this is the right platform for you in which you can embellish your career in your desired way!

Every year the number of applicants aspiring to get admissions in this course has been increasing. One of the main reasons is the decent career prospects that this course offers. Students clearing class 12th and are aspiring to pursue B. Tech or B.E will definitely have confusion as to which branch to choose. Most often computer science turns out to be the primary option for students. Students who are planning to go abroad for higher studies can opt for this course or degree which will be most beneficial for them if anyone wants to start their own business or venture in the field of software or IT.

B.Tech Computer science engineering is all about identifying the problems and solving the same with the assistance of scientific and mathematical principles. B. Tech in CSE is an academic program with a duration of 4 years which integrates the field of computer science and computer engineering. The program mainly emphasizes on the basics of computer programming and networking further more bifurcations in a plethora of topics. The topics covered under B. Tech are related to algorithms, program design, computation, programing language, program design, computer hardware, computer software etc. It is a comprehensive course in computer systems and applications. Students pursuing CSE will develop competence in areas like programming, Data Base Management System, Network of security, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Robotic Science, cloud computing etc.

Eligibility criteria

Aspirants’ should have passed the Class 10+2 exams from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects. Candidates’ should also have secured minimum aggregate marks of 45% in Chemistry, Physics and Mathematics subjects combined. Admissions in Top Colleges are purely done either on merit basis or entrance test basis.

  • JEE Mains
  • JEE Advanced
  • CET
  • COMEDK
  • BITSAT
  • SRM JEE
  • VITEEE

Various colleges and universities offer admissions to students through entrance tests. The entrance exams are organized at national, state, or university level by the respective authorities. JEE Main is a national level examination regulated by CBSE Board for providing admissions in B. Tech programs offered by top colleges of the country. CET & COMEDK is a state level entrance examination test and Private University, Deemed-to-be-University or autonomous university has their own entrance and all this entrance exam tests are conducted to give opportunity to deserving candidates. While some other institutions give admissions on the basis of merit list of the qualifying examination, for admissions in B.Tech course.
