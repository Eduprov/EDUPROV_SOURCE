Bachelor of Commerce Hons

This globe evolves with commerce. Commerce can be simply termed as trade. There is no such record of when this trade started, but now it has become the backbone of a business. Commerce deals with the trade-in large scale. Understanding commerce is very important for every business. B.Com (Hons) is an undergraduate degree that includes a research dissertation. Commerce is present in each and every sector and in every department. So the demand for the candidate who completes this course is high. In this program, you will gain the knowledge and skill, how to meet all upcoming challenges of the business environment, develop your ability to understand and analyze critical merging issues of business and be exposed to intellectual research in the national and global business field. The added advantage of this course is the specialization of subjects like economics and accounting.

In this three-year undergraduate course, one will know about the Economics, principles of accounting, statistics, economic theory, business studies, banking law, finance, management studies, etc... The knowledge you gain in learning the above-mentioned subjects in the six semesters will lead to a bright future.

Eligibility

Every applicant who likes to do this course should fulfill the eligibility condition mentioned by the particular institutions. The basic qualification is 10+2. Every institution has its own cutoffs for admission, which completely depends on the number of students applied for this course. The student must have studied any one of the subjects such as accountancy, business mathematics Economics or Maths in 10+2. A science student can't apply for this course. Further assistance and complete guidance for each and every level will be provided by the EDUPROV experts. You don't need to worry about any process when the complete guidance of your course is in our hands.

Scope

After successful completion of B.Com (Horns), one can work in both the public and private sectors. One has a wide range of options in various industries. The roles include Accountant, Tax Consultant, Human Resource, Banker, BPO / KPO, Auditor, Stock Broker, Lecturer, Export-Import Manager, Finance Consultant, Insurance Consultant, Event Manager, Travel Agent / Travel Manager, Market Researcher, Entrepreneur, Hotel Management, Economist, Administrative Job, etc.

Advanced options such as Chartered Accountant (CA), Company Secretary (CS), and Cost and Work Accountant (CWA) are also available for the student who is successful in their course with good grades. You can apply for the exams while you are at College.

Like India, abroad also has a wide range of job opportunities with an awesome salary package. Many Indians are working in the UK, USA, Canada, South Africa, etc as CA, CS, or CWS. If you want to do advanced studies abroad, then you must keep your student visa and passport for further studies.
