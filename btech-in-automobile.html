B.Tech Automobile Engineering

Automobile Engineering is a field of vehicle engineering; it includes elements of mechanical, electronic, electrical, software and safety engineering, which are applied in designing, manufacturing, and operation of automobiles, bus, truck, motorcycles, and their relevant engineering subsystems. Automobile engineering is also known as Automotive Engineering. It has some specialized fields, such as Product Engineering, Product Engineering, and Manufacturing Engineering. In this field, you will learn about the strength of materials, the theory of machines, power units and transmission, transport management, and the Automobile industry and engine components laboratory.

The main object of Automobile Engineering is to train the student to design, manufacture, and repair in an effective manner and analyses and create or develop new innovative technological advancements in the automotive field.

Eligibility

  • To become an automobile engineer the student must need to get above 50% mark in 10+2 with Mathematics, Chemistry, and Physics.
  • They need to clear the state and national level entrance exam, Common Entrance Exam (CET), and All India Engineering Examination (AIEEE) respectively.
  • B.Tech or BE degree holders in Mechanical or Electrical can also go for specialization in Automobile Engineering at the Master level. provide the course will be explained in detail by the experts.

Scope

Lots of job opportunities are available for Automobile engineers and they can start their career in the automobile industry leads to a bright future. Many Indian and international automobile companies are leading in India. The graduates in automobile Engineering are eligible to get employment in these companies. The candidates who dream to develop a career in the automobile industry with BMW, Volkswagen, Ford, Mercedes, Audi, etc targeting India as a base for manufacturing cars.

You can work in a company as a Quality Engineer, Mechanical Design Engineer, Technical Advisor, Academic Researcher, Underbody Engineer, Supplier Warranty Recovery Specialist, Project leader, Teacher, etc.
