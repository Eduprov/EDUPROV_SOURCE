Bachelor of Optometry (BOPTM)

Course

As the name suggests, optometry and vision science is a profession that deals with the examination of the visual system and the health issues related to it. The Bachelor of Optometry and Vision Science deals with the study of the health issues related to vision. Here you study the function and structure of the visual system, the skills required for clinical assessment, and standards of practice. This course will give you the knowledge and training regarding finding the visual related issues, treatment needed to be given, and all management practice with regards to it. Optometry also involves proper detection of the abnormalities in the visual systems and giving the best diagnosis for the same. Optometry is well recognized in many of countries. So, like several other professionals in healthcare, the optometrists also have to go through several optometrist related courses. The Bachelor of Optometry and Vision Science is a course that is well recognized.

Eligibility

  • To pursue a course of Bachelor of Optometry and Vision Science, one should have completed 10+2 with a Science background with the subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English compulsory.
  • Also, there are several pre-entrance examinations that one needs to give. Once the entrance exam is cleared properly one can take admission for this course.
