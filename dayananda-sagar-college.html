Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering

Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering is situated in Bangalore in Karnataka state of India. Established in 1979, it is accredited from AICTE, NBA DSCE campus is spread over 44 Acres. Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Academic Zone, A/C, AV Lab, Boys Hostel, Canteen, Computer Lab, Planning lab, Guest house, Gym, Library are also there. 'Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering' accepts Karnataka PGCET - Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test for admission into Engineering courses
