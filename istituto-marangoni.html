Istituto Marangoni

Istituto Marangoni offers preparatory courses, one-year courses, three-year courses and semester courses in both fashion and design. They also offer postgraduate courses. Apart from regular courses, Istituto Marangoni also offers shorter two weeks, three weeks and monthly courses to interested individuals. This aids students increase their skills and prepare better for newer job opportunities.
