B.Tech Sound Engineering

Sound Engineering is the field of engineering that deals with music, mixing and reproduction, recording, and manipulating. The course itself is a changeling and needs more than creativity. To pursue this course a candidate should be having a deep understanding of music. The work of a sound engineer is to examine the sound for an event, recording sound and music, speech, and sound. Whenever there is an event a sound engineer is called to check the sound setup. They are responsible to check about the flow sound throughout the area. The Sound Engineering course is available only in certain institutions. The list of institutions and their details that will provide this course will be provided to you by the experts of EDUPROV. This course has a high scope in the media industry. This industry is a booming industry in the current scenario and doing this course is a wise choice.

Unlike other engineering courses, sound engineering can be completed in three years with six semesters. The one who opts for this course will learn about the studio equipment, electronic music production, film sound, electronic music production, live sound, advanced digital technology, studio design, and acoustic environment and several other software like Logi pro, Nudeon, Pro tools, etc. Once when you complete the six semesters the person will be awarded a degree and is known as a sound engineer. If you are willing to know more and love to join the course, then you can approach the dedicated professionals of EDUPROV who are always waiting to assist you.

Eligibility

Bachelor’s Course: The duration of the course is for 3 years and the minimum age to get admission is 17 years. The minimum qualification to get a seat in a college is to pass 12th with English, Physics, and Chemistry as main subjects.

Master’s Degree:This course is for 2 years and there is no specific age limit to avail of this course. The minimum qualification to acquire this course is to have a bachelor’s degree. A bachelor’s degree can also avail a master's degree from foreign educational institutions.

Scope

After pursuing the degree a person can work in movies, advertisement firms, multimedia designs, tv channels, animations, studios, radio stations and much more. They can even start a recording studio where singers and musicians will record their music. The job roles in the course are Studio Technician, Multimedia Developer, Digital Remastering Engineering, Assistant Engineering, Studio Engineer and much more.

Sound Engineers are needed everywhere around the world. There are various world tour programs that require a professional to check the sound. Getting a job anywhere around the world is not a big problem
